log in and sign up requests

Delays

It can take more than 50 seconds for a log in or sign up request to be recieved and processt by the servers depending on were you live.

The process explained

The processing process has to travel cross the world 3 times and has to make stops 4 times.

The journey begins wen you sign up or log in were the information needs to be sent to renderes servers that process it and check with a MongoDB database if the user already exists or if the name is okay after that the servers need to return the result to your computer which could take a long time to depending on were you live.
